# Product photography

Drop product images here, matching the filenames referenced in
`assets/data/products.js` (e.g. `basmati.jpg`, `sunflower.jpg`, `icumsa45.jpg`, …).

Direction (see brief §2.6): rich, warm, editorial shots on dark or sand
backgrounds — overflowing jute sacks of rice, golden oil pours, vivid spice
mounds, sugar crystals catching light, fruit crates, ports at golden hour.

Guidelines:
- Aspect ~4:3, ≥1200px wide, compressed (WebP/JPEG), consistent warm grade.
- Every product needs a descriptive `alt` (set in the data file).
- Until images are added, cards display the alt text gracefully (no broken UI).
